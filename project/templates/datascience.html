eCommerce Customers - Linear Regression
The challenge - With the data provided advise the company where to focus their development resource either on the app or website
Conclusion - Focus on the app as this is currently driving sales with a 38.59 increase in spend per single unit increase compared to the website only generating 0.19.
This said it will not be as clear cut as this. Given the website seems to be performing well below the app, it could have an inherent issue which is driving consumers to the app as a route to purchase. This will cause a loss on conversion which needs to be investigated further to understand why and improve the customer experience on the website.

Student Pass Rate - Tensor Flow/Neural Network Logistic Regression
The Challenge - Predict whether a student will pass or fail a course given metrics on the student.
Conclusion - The model was able to predict a person was going to pass the course but performed much worse on predicting whether or not a person was going to fail the course.
